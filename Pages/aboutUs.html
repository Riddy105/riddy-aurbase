What is aurbase ?

Aurbase is a data-science driven deep hiring platform that accelerates the engineering and design team of companies at the push of a button. At Aurbase we make the remote hiring journey easy and rewarding for both companies, developers and designers.

What does aurbase do?

Aurbase makes remote hiring easy and rewarding for both companies and developers. We enable companies to hire pre-vetted, remote software talent across 100+ skills in days, and provide opportunities for remote developers from all over the world by placing them in high-quality software jobs with top companies.

How does Aurbase work?

Our Intelligent Talent Cloud employs AI to vet, match, and manage over a million developers throughout the world. As a result, organizations can save time and money by assembling their dream tech and design team in a matter of days.

Our story

Due to numerous advantages that fully remote teams provide, practically every IT company is now a remote organization. Companies that have made the transition to remote work are now thriving and have a significant recruiting advantage. Instead of recruiting from a small pool of developers near their offices, companies that go remote have access to a global pool of engineers. They are able to not only hire higher-quality employees, but also keep them for longer periods of time. However, establishing a remote-first organization isn't simple.
